he India Meteorological Department (IMD) on Thursday predicted a wet spell with isolated heavy to very heavy rainfall very likely to continue over south Peninsular India till May 22. Meanwhile, isolated extremely heavy rainfall is also very likely over Tamil Nadu on May 16 and 20 and in Kerala on 20 May. Meanwhile, southwest monsoon is likely to advance into the South Andaman Sea, some parts of Southeast Bay of Bengal and Nicobar Islands around May 19.
